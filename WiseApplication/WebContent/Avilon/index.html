Module Features

Attendance

In WISE, regularly attending classes are critical for the student’s effective learning and success.For every module and a minimum of 90% attendance is must for a student to qualify for the end assessment,if a student is not able to attend the class, proper intimation has to be given to the trainer and approval taken.

Learning Outcomes

Learn new technologies and skills,Acquire a strong knowledge of modern programming platforms and practices,Work on distributed development project teams,Communicate and present technical ideas effectively,Comfortably deal with large code-bases, and Leverage the above to succeed in campus selection.

Scoremore

WISE follows Experiential Learning. In this methodology participants will get online access to learning resources like Handouts, videos, Worksheets, Guided Assignments for self learning.

Assessments

Assessment is conducted at the end of every Module.The Exams have a cut off of which has to be secured by the students. The details will be shared by the trainers.
