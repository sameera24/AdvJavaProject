About WISE

WISE (Women in Software Engineering) is a training program that has been especially designed and created by TalentSprint for women engineering students. The program has been created to enable students to work with mid size software systems by experiential learning. Participants will acquire a deep knowledge of modern programming platforms and practices.
The curriculum combines academic learning with exposure to industry relevant tools, using case studies and assignments. The focus is to build a complement of skills to make the participants confident and competent in the IT product industry and develop a resume with verifiable expertise and experience, relevant for multinational software organizations.
